About Us

We, the female students of Adham University College affiliated with Umm Al-Qura University, in our graduation project have chosen to create a website specialized in culture and tourism for the six Gulf Cooperation Council countries, with a focus on the development taking place in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia as a result of Vision 2030 and its efforts to promote tourism. The website aims to enhance cultural awareness and promote unique tourism experiences in these wonderful countries. .
